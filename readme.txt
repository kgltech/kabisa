Project Name: Kabisa Coffee Shop
Location: Gisement, Kigali, Rwanda

Project Overview:
Kabisa Coffee Shop is a premium coffee brand based in Kigali. The goal is to create a modern, elegant, and high-end website that reflects a true luxury coffee experience. The design should feel rich, clean, and sophisticated, targeting customers who value quality, comfort, and style.

Brand Identity:

Style: Luxury, minimal, and refined
Mood: Warm, welcoming, and premium
Target Feel: Exclusive but comfortable

Brand Colors:

Deep Blue: #003049
Rich Red: #D62828
Warm Orange: #F77F00
Soft Gold: #FCBF49

Typography & Design Direction:

Use clean, modern fonts with a premium feel
Strong contrast with elegant spacing
High-quality visuals (coffee, interior, lifestyle)
Smooth layout with balanced white space

Slogan:
"Crafted for Taste. Designed for Luxury."

Main Goal:
Build a visually stunning website that presents Kabisa Coffee Shop as a top luxury coffee destination in Kigali.
