Our Story

Redefining coffee culture in the heart of Rwanda.

A Taste of Luxury

Situated in the vibrant neighborhood of Gisement, Kigali, Kabisa Coffee Shop was born out of a profound passion for exceptional coffee and sophisticated design. We believe that a cup of coffee is more than just a drink; it's an experience.

Our beans are meticulously sourced from the finest farms in Rwanda, roasted to perfection, and brewed with precision. Every detail of our shop, from the warm lighting to the comfortable seating, is curated to provide you with an exclusive yet welcoming atmosphere.

Crafted for Taste. Designed for Luxury.
